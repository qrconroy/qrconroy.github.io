ITIS 3135

Website Evaluations

Drudge Report

  • Contrast: This website has a strong color contrast with a white background and black text, but the entire website is monochromatic and lacks any variety in color or design.
  • Repetition: The repetition of black text on a white background is consistent throughout the site; however, it is too repetitive and nothing in particular draws attention to any specific element.
  • Alignment: Very poor. Once again, it is consistent; however, everything is left-aligned and lacks visual hierarchy.
  • Proximity: Also very poor. Too much negative space disconnects elements within the same section and the proximity of each section to each other is too small.

.monks

  • Contrast: Very good use of contrast. Usually utilizes a light background with a dark shade of the same color for the text.
  • Repetition: Repetition is used effectively in the design of the website. Elements and color scheme are repeated consistently throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Well aligned. The website uses a consistent and balanced layout with proper alignment of elements.
  • Proximity: Good use of proximity. Elements are grouped together logically and there is appropriate spacing between sections.
