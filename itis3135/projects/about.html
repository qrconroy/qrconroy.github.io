🎯 Our Mission

We believe that everyone can learn to code. The problem isn't people — it's that most coding resources are written by experts, for experts. They're dense, intimidating, and full of jargon.

HayMo is different. We write every lesson as if we're explaining it to a curious friend who has never written a single line of code. We use everyday analogies, colorful examples, and lots of interactive exercises.
